Beksultan Maatkulov

Photo of Beksultan

Who He Helps

Helping students prepare for CEnT-S with clarity and structure

Beksultan Maatkulov teaches students who want to apply to Italian universities and need focused preparation for CEnT-S. His content combines exam explanation, solved tasks, preparation strategy, and self-study support.

Through Instagram and YouTube, he shares lessons, guidance, and practical explanations that help students understand what to study and how to approach the test.

Teaching Approach

What students get from his preparation style

01

Step-by-step explanations

Topics are explained in a simple way so students understand both the theory and the solving process.

02

Exam-focused training

Preparation is organised around actual CEnT-S sections, timing, and strategy, not only around theory.

03

Independent study support

Students who cannot attend everything live can still follow a structured self-study path using the prepared materials.
